Our Mission

The Healthcare Sector needs professionals that are as passionate about cybersecurity as healthcare delivery organizations are regarding patient care. We believe in protecting healthcare delvery organizations' patients, infrastructures, and reputations, so they can focus on saving lives and taking care of people.

BlueNeek Solutions brings a passion to the industry that is sorely lacking among other consulting firms.

BlueNeek Solutions aims to save lives. Emphasis upon the fragility of human life heightens when a threat actor takes down an entire hospital with ransomware, causes rerouted ambulances, and loss of life in transit. Threat actors don't have boundaries, and they'll attack if they can make a profit.
